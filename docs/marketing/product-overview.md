# Product Overview

CE FranchiseOwner is an all-in-one web app built for Commission Express
franchise owners. It brings three jobs together on one screen:

1. **Lead Engine** — automated outbound outreach to real-estate agents whose
   listings just went Active Under Contract.
2. **Call Tracker** — a daily call worksheet for previous and inactive clients,
   so no follow-up slips through the cracks.
3. **Gate Check** — a pre-underwriting triage that scores inbound
   commission-advance application emails *before* you spend time on them.

It runs in your web browser — nothing to install — and you sign in with your
own franchise login.

## Who it's for

- **Franchise owners** who want their day-to-day busywork automated: finding
  fresh agents, emailing them on a schedule, tracking calls, and filtering out
  applications that aren't worth underwriting.
- **The national office**, which can stand up a new franchise's setup in minutes
  and see network-wide reporting.

## What makes it different

- **Automated, but you stay in control.** The app drafts and queues everything;
  you decide who gets contacted, what's said, and when it sends. Nothing goes out
  without your approval.
- **One platform, many branches.** Each franchise (South Carolina, Dallas,
  Virginia, New Mexico, North Carolina, …) sees only its own data. The national
  admin can onboard a new branch — copying South Carolina's proven templates and
  settings — in a few clicks.
- **Built around real engagement.** The dashboard treats every human response —
  an application, a reply, even an unsubscribe — as a signal worth tracking,
  because it means a real person engaged.
- **No data left behind.** Listings, agents, emails, calls, and applications are
  all stored, versioned, and searchable, with full backups you control.

## The payoff

More leads in your pipeline. More past clients converted from calls. And about
30 minutes saved on every single application you don't have to underwrite by hand.
