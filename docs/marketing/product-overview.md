## What CE FranchiseOwner is

A Windows desktop app for Commission Express of South Carolina that:

- Scrapes PropStream for Active Under Contract listings across South
  Carolina counties
- Fuzzy-matches the listing agents on those properties against a
  master roster of licensed SC real-estate agents
- Sends personalised drip-email campaigns to those agents, escalating
  the cadence by contact count

## Who it's for

Commission Express franchise owners who want a hands-off lead
pipeline: load the master agent list once, set up county searches,
and let the system surface new agents to reach out to as listings
come on the market.
